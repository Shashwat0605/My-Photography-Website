About

Hey guy's my name is SHASHWAT SHAURYA. I'm 16 years old, a multi-discipline photographer who also loves painting. I like capturing Wildlife, Nature & Landscape. I share my vision with the world in the hope of inspiring a deeper connection between viewers & mother nature. I began as a young, enthusiastic phone photographer, in Ranchi, Jharkhand (India) and later on, developed and enhanced my skills with the help of my elder brother, and by following some great artists in this field. Sudhir Shivaram, Varun Aditya and Subhash Nair and many more.. After trying on different subjects, I finally found my interest & emerged as a wildlife & nature photographer. Hope my snaps have excited you.

Thank You!!
